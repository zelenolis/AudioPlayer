What it is?

It is a task for rs-school involving the creation of an audio player. The goal is to produce a functional audio player that meets the desired requirements and user needs, whether for personal use, educational purposes, or entertainment.

What can it do?

You can play your mp3 audiofiles. Organize or customize your playlist. Listen your music everywhere

Absolutely, we are committed to continuously improving this application. You can expect regular updates and improvements to make your experience even better in the future.
